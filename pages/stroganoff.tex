\section{Stroganoff}

Cook \ing{1 pound wide egg noodles}
while doing the rest of this.

In a large skillet:
\ing{1 pound ground beef},
\ing{1 chopped onion},
\ing{1/8 cup butter}.

Cook and stir until onion is tender and beef is brown.

Add
\ing{2 tablespoons flour},
\ing{1 teaspoon seasoned salt},
\ing{8 ounces mushrooms}.
Cook 5 minutes, stirring constantly.

Add \ing{10.5-ounce can condense cream of chicken soup}.
Stir. Simmer 10 minutes.

Add \ing{1 cup sour cream}. Stir and heat.

Combine with noodles.
